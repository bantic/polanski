POLLanski: Jail him or Free him?

A surprise arrest of Roman Polanski in Zurich airport on Sep. 27 2009 for the rape of a 13 year old girl in 1977 has caused much debate around the world.

On one side, Polanski's defenders argue he should be freed immediately because the case is too old and the victim has requested the case should be dropped.

On the other side, Polanski's non-sympathizers argue he should be brought back to America and be tried just like any other sex offenders regardless of how old the case is.
